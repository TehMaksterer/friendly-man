Dr. Rickbir Singh Bahia

Dr. Rickbir Bahia is a prominent figure in the field of planetary science, known for his groundbreaking research and contributions to the understanding of the river channels on Mars. His contributions to research have been utilised in the private sector providing guidance to Rolls-Royce on their aeroplane engines across the world. His research has encompassed a diverse range of topics, including the geological history of Mars. Dr Bahia is an accomplished bassist having performed across Europe including cities rich in music such as Manchester, Leeds, and Amsterdam.

Dr Gregory Farquhar

Greg is a DPhil student at the University of Oxford under the supervision of Shimon Whiteson. Previously, Greg obtained a Masters in Physics (1st class honours) from the University of Oxford. Greg was a former intern of NASA JPL and Facebook AI Research. His research is about learning optimal sequential decision making from data, primarily in the framework of reinforcement learning (RL). Key areas of focus include cooperative multi-agent RL, learning to plan, curriculum learning, and (any-order) gradient estimation.
